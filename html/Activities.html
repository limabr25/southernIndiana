Big Four Bridge

A great addition to the Kentuckiana area is the Big Four Bridge across the Ohio River from Jeffersonville, IN to Louisville, KY.The pedestrian bridge is accessible both on the Indiana and Kentucky sides. On any day, many enjoy the excercise and great views of the river and city. There is also a playground at the base of the bridge where kids can play. The recent installation of LED lights make the bridge something to see at night. This would be a great outing with kids, on a date or with family visiting from out of town. Many great restaurants, ice cream shops and stores are popping up within walking distance from the bridge. Dogs are not allowed, but bicycles and strollers are. Overall, it is a great, unique and FREE activity!

Holiday World

Free parking, drinks and sunscreen are available to anyone visiting Holiday World. General Admission is $44.95, Under 54'' is $34.95 and children under 2 are Free! Discount tickets can be purchased at Kroger. Discounts on admission can be obtained throughout the Summer by bringing any empty Pepsi can or Bunny Bread wrapper to the ticket booths for a discount on up to 8 admission tickets (per can or wrapper). There are also discount coupons available at many locations in the area. Holidog's FunTown has a huge playground, a special play area for very young children and rides including the Doggone Trail and a roller coaster. Other areas of the park are themed for various holidays. There are wooden rollercoasters and the newest steel roller coaster, Thunderbird. Splashin' Safari offers some great options for young children including a splash pad, two pools specifically for kids and several small water slides. There is a wave pool, several waterslides and the Wildebeast and Mammoth, which are water coasters. There are family changing rooms with diaper changing tables and family restrooms readily available. You can also rent a locker for $5 with a $5 deposit or a Cabana for $95 per day which includes a secure place for your things, water and chairs to relax in.
